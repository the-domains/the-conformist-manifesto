Creativity and Self Reflection

The Creativity and Self-Reflection Worksheet has made me realize that my main creative barriers have to do with procrastination and a disorganized process for creating ideas. While I'm usually open minded, I usually enter a discussion with preconceived thoughts and ideas and this prevents me from easily assimilating new information. This also leads to some people thinking that I'm closed minded.
